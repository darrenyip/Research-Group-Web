About ES Group

Dr. Xue's group is investigating complex interactions in infrastructure-human-natural systems, and developing sustainable solutions to infrastructure, water, energy and nutrient challenges. We've developed system based approaches to evaluate sustainable and resilient water & waste infrastructure, to analyze environmental and human health impacts of food and biofuel supply chains, and to advance interdisciplinary tools for sustainability and resilience assessment.
